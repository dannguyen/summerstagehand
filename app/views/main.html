About SummerStageLove

STAGEORAMA was built by Dan Nguyen of Skift for the 2013 Hack SummerStage Challenge. It is not officially endorsed by the New York City Parks Foundation nor the SummerStageTK

I built this website because I wanted a fast simple way to see all the events that are scheduled this summer. But after doing that, I realized there were a lot of performers who I hadn't either seen or heard of before, so I used the YouTube API to discover shows that I might not have been interested in if I hadn't seen their TK performance. SummerStageLove contains more than 200x videos related to the shows here. I hope you find the information helpful in planning out your summer!

So if you find this app useful, please consider voting for it in the Hack SummerStage Challenge. If you have any questions/complaints, feel free to contact me on Twitter at @dancow or at my blog
