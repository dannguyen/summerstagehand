About

STAGEORAMA was built by Dan Nguyen of Skift for the 2013 SummerStage Hack Challenge. It is not officially endorsed by the New York City Parks Foundation nor the SummerStageTK

This app was built to create a quick, clean way to navigate the SummerStage 2013 events listing. And for the musicians and artists you haven't seen yet, the VideoRama feature lets you experience the SummerStage festival through hand-curated Youtube videos of the participating acts.

If you like this app, I'd appreciate it if you'd vote in the HackSummerStage Challenge. If I win, the city parks officials will be forced to try to pronounce my last name at the beginning of each event :)
