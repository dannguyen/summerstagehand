About SummerStageLove

I made this website because I wanted a fast simple way to browse the events at SummerStage 2013. I already have apps to find where the parks and the train stops are, so SummerStageLove is focused just on making it easier to discover music. I'm not very good at figuring out music by reading about music, so in addition to creating a easily readable list, I collected more than a hundred YouTube videos for this year's SummerStage performers.

If you're in New York this summer, you might find SummerStageLove useful too. The summer's already short enough without spending time just looking for things to see and do.

This site would not have been possible without the APIs and services of the City Parks Foundation, UPlanMe, ChallengePost, Amazon AWS, Foursquare, Google Maps, and of course, YouTube.

If you do like SummerStageLove, feel free to vote for it in the HackSummerStage Challenge. If you have any questions or comments, feel free to contact me on Twitter at @dancow or at my blog.

– Dan Nguyen
