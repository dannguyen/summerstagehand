About

STAGEORAMA was built by Dan Nguyen of Skift for the 2013 Hack SummerStage Challenge. It is not officially endorsed by the New York City Parks Foundation nor the SummerStageTK

The main reason I made this app was because I wanted a faster way to look at the listings. Also, I don't know all of the musicians, so I wanted a way to preview their work. STAGEORAMA has more than 100 hand-curated videos from participatin acts.

The second reason for this app was because I wanted to win the HackSummerStage Challenge. There's no cash prize, but one of the TKperks is that the winner will be "acknowledged in stage announcements at every SummerStage show," which means maybe someone will pronounce my last name, possibly like this.

So if you find this app useful, please consider voting for it in the Hack SummerStage Challenge. If you have any questions/complaints, feel free to contact me on Twitter at @dancow or at my blog
